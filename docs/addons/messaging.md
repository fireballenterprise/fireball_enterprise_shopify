# Messaging
Shopify's first-party email and SMS marketing app — formerly **Shopify Email**, renamed when
SMS support was added. Campaigns and marketing automations run from the admin. Distinct from
the **Inbox** live-chat sales channel (`docs/sales_channels/inbox.md`).
